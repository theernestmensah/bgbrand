Policies & Information

Shipping Policy

We offer complimentary shipping on all orders over GH₵ 4,500. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Expedited options are available at checkout. All orders are processed within 24 hours of receipt. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped.

Return Policy

We accept returns of unworn, unwashed, and undamaged items within 14 days of delivery. All original tags must be attached. To initiate a return, please initiate a return request through your account portal or contact our customer care team.

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days of receiving your return.
