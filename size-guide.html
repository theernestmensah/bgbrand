How to Measure

For the most accurate fit, we recommend measuring yourself in your undergarments.

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure horizontal and level under your arms.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, which is usually the narrowest part of your torso, typically above your belly button.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips and bottom, keeping the tape measure level.
